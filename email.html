Commencement details for Fall 2025 Undergraduate Ceremony

2025 Fall Commencement

Friday, December 12, 2025, at 10:00 a.m.
at Stegeman Coliseum

Congratulations! The University of Georgia campus community looks forward to welcoming you and your guests to Stegeman Coliseum to celebrate your graduation at the Fall 2025 Undergraduate Commencement Ceremony!

Please share this email with your Commencement guests as it contains helpful and important information regarding the ceremony.

Doors open at 8:00 a.m.


Graduates should arrive no later than 9:15 a.m.


Graduates should enter Stegeman Coliseum from Carlton Street and enter through the doors closest to the crosswalk near the Science Learning Center.

The Clear Bag Policy will be in effect for Commencement.


Weapons are not allowed inside Stegeman Coliseum. A weapon detection system has been implemented to help enhance safety at the venue. Each guest and graduate will be required to pass through the system prior to scanning their ticket and entering the Coliseum. We encourage you to arrive early to allow time for a smoother entry process. Doors open at 8am.


For more information on items that may slow down the screening process, please see the commencement website.

Tickets are required for both guests and graduates.


Graduates and guests should be prepared to scan their digital ticket upon entry.

There will be no processional for the ceremony.


Graduates will sit by their school/college.

Parking for the ceremony is free and available on a first-come, first-served basis.


Suggested parking areas include South Campus Deck (S11), Carlton Street Deck (S15), and Foley Field Lot (S14). McPhaul Lot (S10) and Coverdell Lot (S16) are reserved for designated participants by permit only.


For more information on parking, please visit the Commencement homepage.

Visitors with disability concerns are strongly encouraged to arrive early.


Main entrances to Stegeman Coliseum along Sanford Drive and Carlton Street are wheelchair accessible.

The ceremony will be broadcast live on the Commencement homepage starting at 9:55 a.m.


Road Closure Information: Please be advised that there will be a road closure in front of Stegeman Coliseum to allow for pedestrian traffic only. View entrance map.

Once again, congratulations on your upcoming graduation from the University of Georgia!

You are receiving this message because you have applied to graduate.
